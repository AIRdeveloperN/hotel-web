Our Hotel

Welcome to our website. The NAZIRA Boutique hotel was opened in 2019. We are very pleased to serve you. We hope you will feel at home in our beautiful and cozy family hotel.

Location

Our building is located in the heart of the ancient city and covered with museums, restaurants and madrasas. The Khans castle and the Main squere of the ancient city are next to our hotel. We have terrace with really beautiful view and you can make tea or coffee there. Our rooftop is place where you can watch panoram of the whole city. Terrace photos
